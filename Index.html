Family Support Program

Because healing isn't just for the individual — it's a journey the whole family takes together.

We're Here for You Too

Supporting Families Through Recovery

Addiction affects the entire family system. Our specialized family support program empowers loved ones with the tools, knowledge, and emotional support to become active participants in recovery.

💬

Communication Guidance

We help families learn healthy, non-enabling communication patterns that support recovery rather than hinder it.

  • Active listening techniques
  • Non-judgmental dialogue skills
  • Conflict de-escalation
  • Expressing concern constructively
  • Setting loving limits
🛡️

Emotional Boundaries

Understanding and establishing healthy emotional boundaries is critical for both the recovering individual and the family.

  • Identifying enabling behaviours
  • Setting healthy limits with love
  • Managing guilt and resentment
  • Protecting your own wellbeing
  • Codependency awareness
📅

Routine Support

Helping families establish structured, supportive home environments that reinforce sobriety and stability.

  • Creating recovery-friendly routines
  • Healthy meal & sleep schedules
  • Managing triggers at home
  • Encouraging positive activities
  • Celebrating small milestones
🌱

Recovery Awareness

Educating families about addiction as a medical condition and the realities of the recovery journey.

  • Understanding addiction science
  • Recognising relapse warning signs
  • Supporting without enabling
  • Long-term recovery expectations
  • Community resource navigation
